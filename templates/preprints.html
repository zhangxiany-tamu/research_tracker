About Preprints
What are preprints?

Preprints are research papers that have not yet undergone peer review. They allow researchers to share their findings quickly and receive feedback from the scientific community.

Why use preprint servers?
  • Access to the latest research findings
  • Faster dissemination than traditional publishing
  • Early feedback from the research community
  • Free and open access to scientific knowledge
Note: Preprints have not been peer-reviewed. Please evaluate the methodology and conclusions carefully.
